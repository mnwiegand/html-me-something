Why

There are many reasons to make

  • empower myself by figuring out how stuff works
  • design to my taste & needs
  • if I have the funds
  • if I don't have the funds
  • when only a custom fix will do
  • bragging points
  • build a new product or business
  • stave off boredom

What

Some skills or project ideas are more common than others... here are some of mine!

  • Thingies: accessories for yourself, home or gifts
  • Designs: new version of an existing item
  • Reconfigure an item to a new purpose
  • Fixes: fix a broken item
  • Making food to eat.

Where

You'll need some space and tools, possibly electricity and WiFi as well. You may be surprised to realize how close you are to getting started or finishing your newest project with these resources...
